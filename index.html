Our Story

More Than Just a Coffee

Nestled under warm globe lights with a wall of lush greens beside you, ...and a Coffee is a different kind of café. Born from a love for honest food, real coffee, and unhurried conversations.

We set up our home inside a repurposed container space — clean, minimal, and surrounded by nature. Whether you come for your afternoon coffee, a loaded burger, or simply a place to sit and breathe, this is where you belong. Open every day from 1 PM — when you're ready for a break, we're already waiting.
